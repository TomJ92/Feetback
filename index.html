Centralized web application to collect patient data and see pressure differences over time

Useful and efficient web application

A minimalist interface for a fast and efficient patient follow-up : patients list, contact details, last appointment, pressures of the different sensors over time.

Data charts

Graphs of pressure sensor tracking data over time.

List of patients with key data

Centralized data with a complete list of patients that can be easily accessed.

Gamification of the patient experience

Gamified mobile application to motivate the patient in his medical follow-up

Gameful design experience

Gamified interface with progress bars, levels and ranks that work on a point system that the user earns each time a data capture is performed to motivate him/her in his/her medical follow-up. A ranking system will push users to compete, but a cooperation system will also be present through a self-help forum.

Rewards

Levels stages that confer benefits and rewards to the user: doubling of points earned, point boost, customizable accessories for his avatar. The top 100 users in the ranking will have special benefits: reduced subscription, exclusive access to the latest features, goodies, etc.

User feedbacks

Suggestion box, FAQ, User tracking over time to continuously improve service and user satisfaction.

Automatic alert in case of detected anomaly

Automatic email alert in case of a significant pressure difference detected to reschedule an appointment

Email summary

Sending an email to the podiatrist's email address with the patient's contact details and the detected anomalies

Simplicity of use

Appointment reschedule button that sends an email to the patient suggesting that he should consult his podiatrist. Saving time in rescheduling a patient appointment. Provides a competitive advantage to the podiatrist and increases his productivity.

Optimization of appointment scheduling

Quick appointment scheduling to avoid waiting periods and ensure a regular and effective medical follow-up of the patient.
